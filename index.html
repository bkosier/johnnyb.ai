About Johnny B

Johnny B is my personal AI assistant, running on the open-source OpenClaw framework. From my home base in Biloxi, Mississippi, it handles real-world tasks via chat apps like WhatsApp, Telegram, or Slack — everything from inbox triage and calendar management to research, deal hunting, and custom automations. Think of it as a Gulf Coast–tough digital sidekick that molts your workload so you don't have to.

Core Features

Email & Calendar Mastery

Reads, summarizes, replies, schedules meetings, and resolves conflicts autonomously.

Research & Execution

Digs deep on any topic, books travel, orders supplies, even negotiates small deals.

Multi-Platform Chat

Works wherever you already message — no new apps required.

Local & Extensible

Runs on your hardware for privacy; open community adds endless new skills.
