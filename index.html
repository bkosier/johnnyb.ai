About Johnny B

Johnny B is my personal AI assistant, running on the open-source OpenClaw framework. From my home base in Biloxi, Mississippi, it handles real-world tasks via chat apps like WhatsApp, Telegram, or Slack — everything from inbox triage and calendar management to research, deal hunting, and custom automations. Think of it as a Gulf Coast–tough digital sidekick that molts your workload so you don't have to.

Email & Calendar Mastery

How Johnny B Works

  1. Install OpenClaw locally (GitHub guide takes ~15 minutes).
  2. Connect your preferred chat apps and API keys (e.g., Claude, Grok, etc.).
  3. Message Johnny B naturally: “Clear my inbox,” “Find cheapest flight to Atlanta,” “Summarize Mississippi weather alerts.”
  4. It acts — quietly in the background or with your confirmation as needed.

Note: Fully local-first means maximum privacy; no unnecessary cloud dependency unless you choose it for specific skills.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Johnny B secure and private?

Yes — core runs locally on your device. Only API calls you explicitly allow go out (and even those can be proxied or avoided).

What does it cost to run?

OpenClaw itself is free. Costs come only from LLM API usage (e.g., $5–30/month depending on volume) — cheaper than most subscription AIs.

Can I make my own version?

Absolutely. OpenClaw is open-source. Fork it, customize skills, and build your own Johnny-style assistant.
